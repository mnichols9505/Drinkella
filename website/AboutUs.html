We are a small company that firmly believes that all people will want our Drinkella in their lives because everyone has found themselves walking in the rain and thinking, "I'm thirsty right now I wish I could drink the rain water". With the Drinkella, now it is possible to make those weird wishes come true!

We, from AM^2, are all from different places in the US and met up in Salt Lake City, Utah, where we all attend the same college. We became fast friends and decided this product would be a great and useful idea. We created the product for people who like to be eco-friendly and want to fulfill the urge to drink rain water.
