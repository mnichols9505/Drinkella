Special Extensions

Water Heater

Introducing the Water Heater Extension. With the heater extension your natural rain water will be automatically warmed in seconds! Heat up some hot cocoa tea or other liquid beverages you wish to be high in temperatures. Pair with our Infuser Extension and you will be able to enjoy your own tea and coffee.

Infuser

The Infuser is one of our Most Popular Extension because it allows the consumer to pre-insert fresh fruits into the Drinkella official water bottle and once the rain water is collected and filtered our wonderful Infuser extension will combine the fresh flavors of the fruit with the glorious rain water almost instantly. You may combine the the Water Heater Extention with the infuser to enjoy hot drinks such as tea.

Mixer

The Mixer Extension is for the customers who enjoy drinks with added ingredients such as, protein powder, sugar or Kool-Aid, and wish to have them mixed automatically into their rain water. No need for a spoon or shaking the container, just push a button and the sharp blade inside the bottle spins the delicious liquid inside into a perfectly mixed drink.

Ice Maker

Is your originally cold drink getting warm on a hot day in seconds? The Ice Maker Extension for the Drinkella can fix that. By creating its very own supply of cold ice cubes with collected rain water in a matter of minutes. Buy the Ice Maker extension to beat the heat.
